Camping Gear Lists

These are simple gear lists based on different camping environments.

Mountain Gear

  • Tent (weather-resistant)
  • Sleeping bag (rated for colder temps)
  • Sleeping pad
  • Warm layers (jacket, hat)
  • Headlamp or flashlight
  • Portable stove
  • Food and water

Lakeside Gear

  • Tent
  • Sleeping bag
  • Camping chairs
  • Cooler
  • Water shoes or sandals
  • Fishing gear (optional)
  • Bug spray

Forest Gear

  • Tent or hammock
  • Sleeping bag
  • Tarp or rain fly
  • Rope or straps
  • Headlamp
  • Cooking supplies
  • Extra socks and layers

Desert Gear

  • Tent with good ventilation
  • Sleeping bag (lightweight)
  • Lots of water
  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen)
  • Sunglasses
  • Lightweight clothing
  • Portable shade (optional)

Beach Gear

  • Tent or sun shelter
  • Sleeping bag or blanket
  • Cooler
  • Towels
  • Sand stakes
  • Sunscreen
  • Extra water
